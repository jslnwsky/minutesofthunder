Best Battery for Traxxas Slash: 2S & 3S LiPo Picks for 2025

The Traxxas Slash is one of the most popular RC trucks ever made. Whether you run the 2WD, 4x4, or VXL brushless version, the battery you choose directly affects speed, runtime, and how much fun you have at the track or in the backyard.

This guide covers what physically fits the Slash battery tray, which voltage and capacity to choose, and specific pack recommendations that balance performance with value.

What Fits the Slash Battery Tray

The Traxxas Slash battery tray accepts standard stick-style LiPo packs. Key dimensions to check before ordering:

Dimension Slash 2WD Slash 4x4 VXL
Max Length~155mm~155mm
Max Width~47mm~47mm
Max Height~25mm~25mm
Pack StyleStick / ShortyStick / Shorty

Tip: Shorty packs (~96mm long) fit but leave extra space. They work fine, just use the battery hold-down strap to keep the pack secure.

Best 2S LiPo Batteries for the Traxxas Slash

2S (7.4V) is the standard voltage for both the brushed XL-5 and brushless VXL Slash. These picks prioritize capacity, fit, and value.

Zeee 2S 5200mAh 50C — Best Overall Value

5200mAh gives long runtimes (15-20+ minutes bashing). 50C burst rating handles the Slash's current demands easily. Hard case, Deans connector, reliable and affordable.

Fits: Slash 2WD, Slash 4x4 · Connector: Deans (T-Plug)

Gens Ace 2S 5000mAh 50C — Proven Quality

A trusted name in RC LiPo packs. Consistent voltage under load, good cycle life, and available with Deans or EC5 connectors. Slightly more expensive than Zeee but a known quantity.

Fits: Slash 2WD, Slash 4x4 · Connector: Deans or EC5

SMC True Spec 2S 5200mAh 50C — Premium Pick

SMC packs are built for racers. Tight voltage tolerances, reliable IR, and excellent consistency pack-to-pack. Worth the premium if you race or want packs that last hundreds of cycles.

Fits: Slash 2WD, Slash 4x4 · Connector: Deans or 5mm bullets

Best 3S LiPo Batteries (VXL Slash Only)

Warning: Only run 3S in a Slash with the VXL-3s ESC (brushless). The XL-5 ESC on the brushed Slash is not rated for 3S and will be damaged.

3S (11.1V) gives the VXL Slash significantly more speed and acceleration. The tradeoff is shorter runtime and more stress on drivetrain components.

Zeee 3S 5200mAh 50C — Best 3S Value

Same reliable Zeee quality. 5200mAh on 3S gives 10-15 minutes of aggressive driving. Fits the stock battery tray without modification.

Fits: Slash 4x4 VXL · Connector: Deans (T-Plug)

Gens Ace 3S 5000mAh 60C — Performance Pick

Higher C-rating means more consistent voltage delivery under hard acceleration. Good for track use where you want predictable power throughout the run.

Fits: Slash 4x4 VXL · Connector: EC5 or Deans

Traxxas iD vs Aftermarket Packs

Traxxas sells Power Cell LiPo batteries with their iD connector system. These auto-detect on EZ-Peak chargers, which is convenient but comes at a price.

Factor Traxxas iD Aftermarket
Price per mAhHighLow to Mid
Charger CompatibilityEZ-Peak auto-detectAny balance charger
Capacity OptionsLimitedWide range
ConnectorTraxxas iDDeans, EC5, XT60, etc.

The recommendation: aftermarket packs offer significantly better value. If you already own an EZ-Peak charger, you can still use aftermarket packs with a Traxxas-to-Deans adapter, or invest in a dedicated balance charger that works with any pack.

Connectors and Adapters

The stock Traxxas connector is proprietary. When switching to aftermarket batteries, you have two options:

  • Use an adapter: A Traxxas-to-Deans adapter plugs between the ESC and battery. Simple, but adds a small amount of resistance.
  • Solder new connectors: Replace the Traxxas connector on the ESC wire with a Deans (T-Plug) or EC5. This is a one-time job that eliminates adapters permanently.

For casual bashers, an adapter works fine. For racers or anyone who wants the cleanest electrical connection, soldering is the better long-term solution.

Charging Tips for the Slash

  • Charge rate: 1C is the standard safe rate. A 5000mAh pack at 1C = 5A charge current.
  • Always balance charge: Use the balance lead every time. This keeps cells even and extends pack life.
  • Storage voltage: If you are not using the pack for more than a day or two, store it at 3.8V per cell using your charger's storage mode.
  • LiPo bag: Always charge in a LiPo-safe bag or on a fire-resistant surface.

FAQ

What size battery fits the Traxxas Slash?

Standard stick or shorty LiPo packs up to about 155mm x 47mm x 25mm. Both the 2WD and 4x4 accept the same basic form factor.

Can I run a 3S LiPo in a Traxxas Slash?

Yes, but only in the VXL brushless version with the VXL-3s ESC. The brushed XL-5 ESC cannot handle 3S voltage.

Should I use Traxxas iD batteries or aftermarket?

Aftermarket packs offer better value per mAh. Traxxas iD is convenient if you use an EZ-Peak charger, but you pay a premium for the proprietary connector.
