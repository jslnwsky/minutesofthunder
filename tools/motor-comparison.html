      About This Estimate

      This is the theoretical maximum assuming no losses. Real-world speed is typically 70-85% of theoretical due to drivetrain friction, aerodynamic drag, rolling resistance, and motor efficiency under load. For geared vehicles (like most buggies and trucks with a transmission), make sure to include the internal gear ratio.
