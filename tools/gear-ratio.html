Interpret the Result

  • Lower final ratio usually means more top speed and less mechanical advantage.
  • Higher final ratio usually means more torque, cooler motor temps, and slower top speed.
  • Rollout helps compare setups across different tire diameters.
  • Small gearing changes can push motor and ESC temperatures hard, so recheck temps after testing.
